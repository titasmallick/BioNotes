# Our School: A Happy Place

## Key Concepts

### Green School Concept
A "Green School" is a school that takes active steps to protect the environment and manage resources wisely. Key features include:
*   **Waste Management:** Reducing, reusing, and recycling waste.
*   **Water Conservation:** Saving water and fixing leaks.
*   **Electricity Saving:** Using fans and lights only when needed and using natural light.
*   **Green Campus:** Planting trees and creating gardens to attract birds and butterflies.

### Swachh Bharat Swachh Vidyalaya
This is a part of the Swachh Bharat Mission that focuses on keeping schools clean. It emphasizes:
*   Clean toilets and hygiene education.
*   Safe drinking water.
*   Proper waste management.

### Waste Management and Segregation
Waste should be separated at the source to make it easier to manage:
*   **Wet Waste (Green Bin):** Food peels, leftovers, and other organic matter. This can be used to make **compost**.
*   **Dry Waste (Blue Bin):** Paper, plastic, and other non-biodegradable items.
*   **Recyclers:** Items like old newspapers, metal, and glass should be given to traditional recyclers for reuse.

### Keeping the School Cool
Buildings can be kept cool without excessive electricity through:
*   **Shade:** Planting trees near buildings.
*   **Ventilation:** Designing classrooms with windows that allow a breeze.
*   **Reflective Surfaces:** Painting roofs **white** reflects the Sun's heat, while black surfaces absorb it and make rooms hotter.

### Water Conservation
Water is a precious resource that must be used carefully:
*   **Leaking Taps:** Even a small drip can waste many glasses of water in a single day.
*   **Rainwater Harvesting:** Collecting rainwater from roofs and using it for gardening.
*   **Jal Shakti Abhiyan:** A government campaign to save and protect water through tree plantation and rainwater harvesting.

### Biodiversity in School
Trees are essential for a green school because they:
*   Provide shade and keep the surroundings cool.
*   Clean the air.
*   Provide food and home for birds and insects.
*   Help in creating "Butterfly Gardens" by planting species that attract them.

### Safety and Responsibility
A happy school is also a safe and caring school:
*   **Traffic Safety:** Following traffic rules and signs near the school gate to prevent accidents.
*   **Fire Safety:** Knowing where fire extinguishers are kept and practicing fire drills (moving quickly and calmly, crawling low under smoke).
*   **Social Behavior:** Being kind, respectful, and polite. Waiting for one's turn and helping others makes everyone feel included and happy.
