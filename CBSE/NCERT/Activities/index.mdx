# NCERT Biology Activities

Welcome to the comprehensive repository of NCERT Biology Activities. This collection has been meticulously organized class-wise and chapter-wise to help students and educators explore the practical side of biological sciences.

## Explore by Class

### Class 5 EVS
[Explore Activities](./Class_05_EVS) - Focuses on environmental awareness, water ecosystems, and basic health.

### Class 6 Science
[Explore Activities](./Class_06_Science) - Introduction to diversity in the living world, mindful eating, and exploring nature's treasures.

### Class 7 Science
[Explore Activities](./Class_07_Science) - Covers nutrition in plants and animals, respiration, and reproduction in plants.

### Class 8 Science
[Explore Activities](./Class_08_Science) - Explores the invisible living world (microorganisms), harmony in nature, and ecology.

### Class 9 Science
[Explore Activities](./Class_09_Science) - Fundamental concepts of life, tissues, and advances in agriculture.

### Class 10 Science
[Explore Activities](./Class_10_Science) - In-depth activities on life processes, control and coordination, and heredity.

### Class 11 Biology
[Explore Activities](./Class_11_Biology) - Advanced experiments on cell structure and physiological concepts.

### Class 12 Biology
[Explore Activities](./Class_12_Biology) - Higher-level experiments on genetics, evolution, and complex ecology.
