# Exercises

**1. Ramesh, an 11-year-old boy, developed a few pimples on his face.**
*   **(i) Possible reasons:** Increased activity of sweat and sebaceous (oil) glands during adolescence. The oily secretions clog skin pores, leading to acne and pimples.
*   **(ii) Relief:** Maintain personal hygiene, wash the face regularly with mild soap and water, and avoid picking or squeezing the pimples to prevent infection.

**2. Which of the following food groups would be a better option for adolescents and why?**
*   **Answer:** Group (ii) (balanced diet with fruits, vegetables, pulses, and milk) is better than Group (i) (fast food/snacks).
*   **Why:** Adolescence is a period of rapid growth. The body needs essential nutrients like proteins for muscle building, calcium for bones, and vitamins/minerals for overall development, which are found in a balanced diet.

**4. Shalu told her friend, “Adolescence brings only physical changes.” Is she correct?**
*   **Answer:** No, she is incorrect.
*   **Description:** Adolescence brings not just physical changes, but also **emotional**, **biological**, and **behavioural** changes. It involves the development of strong emotions, heightened curiosity, and the maturation of the reproductive system.

**6. What other behavioural changes are associated with this age?**
*   Increased independence, self-consciousness, attraction toward peers, interest in social initiatives, and sometimes confusion or excitement about new experiences.

**7. What menstrual hygiene and sanitary habits would you suggest?**
*   Change sanitary pads every 4–6 hours.
*   Dispose of used pads by wrapping them in paper and putting them in a dustbin (do not throw in open areas).
*   Wash hands with soap after handling pads.
*   Maintain regular body hygiene.
